What is IELTS?

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is a prominent English language proficiency exam recognized globally. Check out the main points about IELTS given below:

  • Language Assessment: IELTS evaluates a candidate's English language skills for academic, professional, and immigration purposes.
  • Test Format: The exam comprises four sections - Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.
  • Test Versions: There are two versions - Academic and General Training, catering to different objectives.
  • Scoring: IELTS uses a nine-band scale to indicate language proficiency levels.
  • Global Acceptance: IELTS scores are accepted by numerous universities, employers, and immigration authorities worldwide.
  • Fair Evaluation: IELTS offers a fair and standardized assessment of language abilities, ensuring consistent results for test-takers.
