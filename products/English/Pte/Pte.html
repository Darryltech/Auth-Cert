What is PTE?

When it comes to what is PTE exam, it is an English language proficiency exam conducted by Pearson Language Tests. It assesses an individual's ability to use English in academic and real- life settings. Have a look at some of the key points about PTE right below.

  • Computer-based Test: PTE is entirely computerized, ensuring fair and unbiased evaluation.
  • Four Sections: It consists of four main sections - Speaking, Writing, Reading, and Listening.
  • Integrated Skills: PTE evaluates a candidate's ability to combine different language skills, mirroring real-life language use.
  • Fast Results: Test-takers receive their scores typically within 5 business days.
  • Widely Accepted: Recognized by thousands of institutions and governments worldwide for study, work, and immigration purposes.
